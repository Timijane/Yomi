About

Pastor Yomi Okewunmi

Minister. Scholar. Author. Mentor. A life dedicated to raising purpose-driven leaders across generations.

Pastor. Scholar. Visionary.

Pastor Yomi Okewunmi, fondly known as PY, is a dynamic minister, scholar, mentor, author, and visionary leader from Abeokuta, Ogun State, Nigeria. He is widely recognized for his impact in ministry, youth empowerment, education, and leadership development across Nigeria and beyond.

He serves as the Resident Pastor of SPAC Church Worldwide, where he has become a respected voice in advancing God's Kingdom through the ministry of the Word and the demonstration of power. His teachings are known for their depth, clarity, and practical relevance to everyday life.

"He is passionate about raising people who walk in purpose, excellence, and spiritual maturity."

Pastor Yomi is the pioneer of the monthly youth gathering Arise Conference, now in its fourth year — a prophetic meeting point for young people discovering their path in God, purpose in life, and place in destiny. He is also the founder of Afrowecanism, an African ideology built on the belief: "As Africans, We Can", and Edutop Scholar, an educational initiative helping students achieve academic excellence.

He is happily married to Dr. Kenny Okewunmi, and they are blessed with two wonderful children.
