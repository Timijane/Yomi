About

A Life of Impact & Purpose

Pastor. Scholar. Visionary.

Pastor Yomi Okewunmi, fondly known as PY, is a dynamic minister, scholar, mentor, and author from Abeokuta, Ogun State. He serves as Resident Pastor of SPAC Church Worldwide, where his teachings are known for their depth, clarity, and practical relevance.

With a First Class Honours degree in Philosophy from the University of Ibadan, a Distinction in his Master's, and a National Diploma with Distinction from The Polytechnic Ibadan, PY embodies the spirit of excellence.

He is the visionary founder of Arise Conference, Afrowecanism, and Edutop Scholar — movements that have empowered thousands across Nigeria and beyond.

Ministry & Movements

Building Lives, Raising Leaders

SPAC Church Worldwide

Resident Pastor advancing God's Kingdom through the ministry of the Word and demonstration of power. Raising people who walk in purpose, excellence, and spiritual maturity.

Arise Conference

Monthly youth gathering now in its fourth year — a prophetic meeting point for young people discovering their path in God, purpose in life, and place in destiny. Equipping a generation for career, entrepreneurship, and leadership.

Afrowecanism

An African ideology built on the powerful belief: "As Africans, We Can." Inspiring young people to embrace their African identity as a blessing, promoting confidence, excellence, and global impact.

Edutop Scholar

Educational initiative helping students achieve academic excellence and unlock their highest potential. Many students have been mentored, guided, and empowered to succeed academically.
